My Story

Hello Everyone! My name is Micah and I am a cyber security student studying in Sheridan College. I want to document my journey and share what I learned here through my blog. I am personally drawn into the red team side of cyber security. If anyone is trying to get in to the Industry, I hope this can help (since I am a newbie myself) and if you have any input for me you can always reach out to my linkedIn Micah Rahardjo.

My eyes is set for the red team eversince I listened to a podcast called Darknet Diaries (I would really recommend all of you to listen to the podcast to!) and one of my favorite epsiodes is episode 61. The podcast talked about an internet worm called Samy, and I remember one of the techniques that Samy use is XSS. Thus, I dug deeper and find a great learning platform called Port Swigger Academy. This is a platform where they teach you different types of vulnerabilities and how to exploit them.
